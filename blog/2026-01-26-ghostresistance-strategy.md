Introducing GhostResistance, a new order-flow reversal strategy built to trade liquidity traps at key support/resistance zones.

## Key Features

- Stacked absorption detection at critical price levels
- Big trade and iceberg order confirmation signals
- Focus on identifying high-probability reversal opportunities following stop-runs and false breakouts

## Risk Management

The tool provides traders with flexible controls for position management, including scaling options, breakeven protection, and trailing stop functionality.
