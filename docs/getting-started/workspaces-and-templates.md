# Workspaces and Templates

MZpack installation puts a set of chart templates and workspaces in corresponding NinjaTrader 8 folders.

## Chart Templates

Check available chart templates by clicking **Templates – Load** from the chart context menu.

## Workspaces

Workspaces are available from the **Workspaces** main menu. The name of each MZpack workspace starts with **MZpack**.

:::note
Loading of some workspaces (e.g., MZpack - VolumeProfiles - Sessions, Weekly, Monthly (30 Minute) with Days to load = 30) might take 30–60 seconds depending on your computer's performance.
:::
